Summoning a Demon Has to be Illegal?

so let’s say i summon a demon. the ritual’s totally legal—no blood, no crime, just some old magic books i found online or whatever. then the demon goes off, wrecks a city, kills like a thousand people, until some exorcists show up and shut it down. i’m like 99% sure that’s illegal but i don’t know what law it breaks exactly. what would they even charge me with? what could a lawyer say to help? and what if i didn’t mean for anyone to get hurt, or didn’t think the demon was real, or it just caused property damage and no one died? would i still be in trouble? morally?? am i just a bad person??? asking for a friend.

Response from User/KileHollows6Six6

Litigation lawyer here. An individual who negligently unleashes a known dangerous entity, whether a wild animal or a supernatural force, may be criminally liable for resulting harm. In many jurisdictions, such conduct could constitute second-degree manslaughter, as it involves death caused by the act, procurement, or culpable negligence of another. The legal principles would similarly apply to the summoning of a hazardous entity without adequate containment measures.
